About me

Hi, I am Ilaria, an MSc graduate in Environmental Monitoring and Assessment based in Rome. After completing my studies I worked for a year as an environmental consultant and a short period as an environmental technician.

While working I became very interested in web development and data analysis. Therefore I decided to join several online courses and bootcamps aimed at strenghtening the data analysis skills that I acquired during my studies at University, as well as learnirng new skills in web development.

Although the journey to become a professional developer is still long, I am very excited to keep learning and keep discovering new ways to build something useful for people and the environment!
